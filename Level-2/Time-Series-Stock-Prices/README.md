## Objective
To analyze stock price data over time in order to identify trends, patterns, and seasonal components using time series analysis techniques.

## Dataset

## Task Performed: Time Series Analysis

### Steps Involved
- Loaded the stock price dataset and inspected its structure
- Converted the date column to datetime format and set it as the index
- Visualized stock price movements over time
- Applied moving average smoothing to identify underlying trends
- Decomposed the time series into trend, seasonality, and residual components

---

## Analysis
- Time series plots helped observe overall price movement and volatility
- Moving averages reduced short-term noise and highlighted long-term trends
- Decomposition revealed underlying trend and seasonal patterns in the data
